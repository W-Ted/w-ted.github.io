Yuxin Wang

I am currently a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Dan Xu, at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I received my Bachelor's Degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019. I worked as an intern and researcher at SenseTime from 2019 to 2022, working closely with Dr. Wayne Wu, Dr. Bo Dai and Dr. Qianyi Wu.

I am interested in the reconstruction and editing of 3D scenes and objects based on NeRF/3DGS.
