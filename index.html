Yuxin Wang

Yuxin Wang is currently a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Dan Xu, at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Yuxin received his Bachelor's Degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019. He worked as an intern and researcher at SenseTime from 2019 to 2021, working closely with Dr. Wayne Wu, Dr. Bo Dai and Mr. Qianyi Wu.

I am interested in recovering 3D structures from videos and multiple images by deep learning methods. I am currently working on object-compositioanl NeRFs.
